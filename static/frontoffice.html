About Esprit University

Excellence in Engineering Education

Esprit is a leading private engineering school in Tunisia, committed to providing world-class education in engineering and technology. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to nurturing the next generation of engineers, innovators, and leaders who will shape the future of technology and society.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional engineering education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical experience, preparing our students to become innovative engineers capable of meeting the challenges of the modern world. We foster creativity, critical thinking, and professional excellence in all our programs.

Academic Programs

Esprit offers comprehensive engineering programs including Computer Science, Telecommunications, Industrial Engineering, and more. Our curriculum is designed to meet international standards while addressing local and regional technological needs.
